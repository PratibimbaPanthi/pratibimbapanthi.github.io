About me

I’m Pratibimba Panthi, a 15-year-old from Nepal with a developing interest in Artificial Intelligence. I began coding through Scratch and later experimented with game development, but my focus shifted once I discovered how AI systems learn, reason, and solve problems. I now spend my time understanding the core ideas behind AI and Machine Learning, studying how models work, how data shapes their behavior, and how Python can be used to build basic intelligent tools. School and homework take most of my day, yet I keep a steady routine for learning AI because it is the field that challenges my thinking the most. This website records my progress, my experiments, and the path I am taking as I grow toward deeper knowledge in AI.
